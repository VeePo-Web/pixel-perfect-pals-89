## About Island Lakes, Manitoba

Island Lakes is a southeast Winnipeg community of about 7,525 people, distinctive for being built around a network of man-made lakes and canals. Developed largely in the 1980s and 1990s, it is a planned residential neighbourhood where many homes sit on artificial waterfront and water-view lots. Its later-era construction, its waterfront setting, and its position on the flat Red River Valley plain and gumbo clay define its residential character.
