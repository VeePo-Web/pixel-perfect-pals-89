## About Carman, Manitoba

Carman is a town of 3,114 people (2021 census) on the Boyne River in the northern Pembina Valley, 76 km southwest of Winnipeg. An agricultural service centre and the seat of the surrounding RM of Dufferin, it anchors a rich seed-and-grain farming district — the University of Manitoba operates a research farm nearby — and hosts the Dufferin Agricultural Society fair. Its farm-service role, its Boyne River setting, and its Red River Valley clay define its residential character.
