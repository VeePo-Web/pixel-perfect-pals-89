## About Portage la Prairie, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Portage la Prairie surrounds — but does not include — the city of the same name, covering 1,973 square kilometres between the Assiniboine River and the south shore of Lake Manitoba. Its 6,888 residents (2021 census) live in communities such as Oakville, High Bluff, St. Ambroise, and Delta Beach, and on farms working the stone-free lacustrine loam of the Portage plains, among Canada's richest soils. The Portage Diversion flood channel crosses the RM, and Delta Marsh, one of North America's great freshwater marshes, lines its lakeshore.
