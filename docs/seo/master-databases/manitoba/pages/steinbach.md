## About Steinbach, Manitoba

Steinbach is Manitoba's third-largest city, with a 2021 census population of 17,806 — up 11.1% from 2016, part of the fastest-growing region in the province. Set 52 km southeast of Winnipeg on well-drained ground east of the Red River Valley, it grew from Mennonite settlement into an entrepreneurial hub known for its automobile dealerships, trucking, and manufacturing, and it is nicknamed the Automobile City. Its Mennonite Heritage Village anchors a strong cultural identity, and its housing runs from settlement-era homes to fast-building new subdivisions.
