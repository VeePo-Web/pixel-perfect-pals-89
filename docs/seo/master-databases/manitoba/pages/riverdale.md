## About Riverdale, Manitoba

Riverdale is an amalgamated municipality of 1,803 people (2021 census) in Westman, Manitoba, centred on the town of Rivers about 47 km northwest of Brandon. Set on the Little Saskatchewan River, it takes in Lake Wahtopanah — the Rivers reservoir — and the site of the former CFB Rivers air base, now an industrial park. Its river-and-lake setting, its Rivers hub, and its mix of town, lake, and farm property define its residential market.
