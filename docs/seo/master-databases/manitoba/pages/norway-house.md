## About Norway House, Manitoba

Norway House is a Cree community of about 5,753 people (2021 census) in northern Manitoba, at the north end of Lake Winnipeg where its waters drain into the Nelson River, 458 km north of Winnipeg. One of Manitoba's largest First Nation communities, it grew from the Hudson's Bay Company's historic inland transport headquarters — a hub of the York boat era — and its heritage buildings still mark that fur-trade past. It sits on Canadian Shield lowlands amid boreal forest and water, with a fast-growing, young population.
