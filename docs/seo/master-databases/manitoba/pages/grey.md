## About Grey, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Grey is a Central Plains municipality of 2,517 people (2021 census) on the south-central Manitoba plains, 70 km southwest of Winnipeg. It takes in the community of Elm Creek and the francophone village of St. Claude, part of Manitoba's French settlement belt. This is grain and mixed-farm country on flat, fertile plains near the western edge of the Red River Valley, worked through deep-frost prairie winters and the freeze-thaw shoulder seasons.
