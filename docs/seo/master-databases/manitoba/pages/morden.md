## About Morden, Manitoba

Morden is a fast-growing town of 9,929 people (2021 census) in Manitoba's Pembina Valley, 105 km southwest of Winnipeg at the toe of the Manitou Escarpment. Winkler's civic twin, it grew 14.5% from 2016 and is known for the Canadian Fossil Discovery Centre — home to Canada's largest marine-reptile collection, including a 13-metre mosasaur — as well as Lake Minnewasta and the Corn and Apple Festival. Its rapid growth, its escarpment-toe setting, and its new-build housing on Red River Valley clay define its market.
