## About Altona, Manitoba

Altona is a town of 4,267 people (2021 census) in Manitoba's Pembina Valley, 93 km southwest of Winnipeg in the Mennonite West Reserve. Known as Manitoba's sunflower capital — it hosts the Manitoba Sunflower Festival and displays the world's largest reproduction of Van Gogh's Sunflowers — it is also home to Friesens Corporation, Canada's largest book printer. Its industrial and agricultural base, its Mennonite heritage, and its valley-floor clay define its residential character.
