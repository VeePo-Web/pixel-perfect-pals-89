## About Morris, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Morris is a Red River Valley municipality of 3,049 people (2021 census) surrounding the town of Morris, 60 km south of Winnipeg. Rich grain and mixed-farm country on the deep valley-floor clay, it takes in communities like Sperling and Rosenort and sits on the flood-prone Red River plain protected in part by the Highway 75 corridor's flood works. Its intensive farm economy, its deep valley clay, and its flood-plain setting define its market.
