## About Cartier, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Cartier lies along the Trans-Canada Highway immediately west of Winnipeg's fringe, with 3,344 residents (2021 census) across 553 square kilometres of flat grain land. Elie, its main town, endured Canada's only officially rated F5 tornado on June 22, 2007, with no loss of life. St. Eustache, near the Assiniboine River, carries the RM's francophone and Métis roots. Soils transition from Red River Valley clay in the east toward Portage plains loam westward, and open exposure makes wind and summer storms defining conditions.
