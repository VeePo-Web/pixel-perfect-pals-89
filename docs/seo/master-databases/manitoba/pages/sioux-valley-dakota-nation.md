## About Sioux Valley Dakota Nation, Manitoba

Sioux Valley Dakota Nation is a Dakota First Nation community of 1,057 people (2021 census) west of Brandon in Westman, Manitoba, on the Assiniboine River. In 2014 it became the first self-governing First Nation in Manitoba under a landmark self-government agreement, taking on jurisdiction over its own affairs.
