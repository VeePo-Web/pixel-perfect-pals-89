## About The Pas, Manitoba

The Pas is a town of 5,639 people (2021 census) in northern Manitoba, 520 km northwest of Winnipeg on the Saskatchewan River. A historic fur-trade site turned rail-and-river gateway, it is the north's transportation and forestry hub, home to the main campus of University College of the North and adjacent to Opaskwayak Cree Nation across the river. The surrounding boreal country includes Clearwater Lake Provincial Park, and the town hosts the Northern Manitoba Trappers' Festival each winter.
