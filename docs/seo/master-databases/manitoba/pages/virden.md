## About Virden, Manitoba

Virden is a town of 3,118 people (2021 census) in southwestern Manitoba on the Trans-Canada Highway, 70 km west of Brandon near the Saskatchewan border. Known as Manitoba's oil capital, it has pumped oil from the Williston Basin since the 1950s, and its sandstone-trimmed main street is a legacy of an earlier boom. A farm-and-oil service town on Westman's sandy till, its oil-and-agriculture economy, its heritage core, and its till ground define its residential character.
