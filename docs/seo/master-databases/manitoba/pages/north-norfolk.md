## About North Norfolk, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of North Norfolk is a Central Plains municipality of 3,915 people (2021 census) on the Trans-Canada Highway between Portage la Prairie and Brandon, 122 km west of Winnipeg. It takes in the towns of MacGregor and Austin, the latter home to the Manitoba Agricultural Museum and the annual Manitoba Threshermen's Reunion. This is table-flat, stone-free plains farmland — grain and mixed agriculture — swept by open-prairie wind and worked through deep-frost prairie winters.
