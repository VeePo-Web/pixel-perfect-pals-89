## About Sagkeeng (Fort Alexander), Manitoba

Sagkeeng, also known as Fort Alexander, is an Anishinaabe community of 2,127 people (2021 census) in eastern Manitoba, at the mouth of the Winnipeg River where it enters Lake Winnipeg, about 99 km northeast of Winnipeg. Named for a historic fur-trade fort, it sits beside the towns of Powerview and Pine Falls on the river's hydro corridor. The community grew 10.3% between 2016 and 2021 and is known nationally as the home of Sagkeeng's Finest, the jigging trio that won Canada's Got Talent in 2012.
