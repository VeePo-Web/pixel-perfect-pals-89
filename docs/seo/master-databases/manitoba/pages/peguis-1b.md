## About Peguis, Manitoba

Peguis is a First Nation community of 3,053 people (2021 census) in Manitoba's northern Interlake, on the Fisher River about 160 km north of Winnipeg. It is the largest First Nation community in Manitoba by population, named for Chief Peguis, and it grew 12.9% between 2016 and 2021. Set on low-lying Interlake plain, the community is well known for recurring Fisher River flooding that has prompted repeated spring responses, which makes drainage and flood-aware property planning a defining local concern.
