## About Dufferin, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Dufferin is a Pembina Valley municipality of 2,543 people (2021 census) surrounding the town of Carman on the Boyne River, 79 km southwest of Winnipeg. One of Manitoba's premier seed-and-grain farming districts — home to a University of Manitoba research farm — it blends intensive agriculture with rural-residential property on Red River Valley clay. Its seed-farm economy, its Boyne River setting, and its valley clay define its market.
