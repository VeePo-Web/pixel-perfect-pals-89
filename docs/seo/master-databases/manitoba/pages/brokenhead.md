## About Brokenhead, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Brokenhead is a municipality of 5,414 people (2021 census) northeast of Winnipeg, surrounding the town of Beausejour and taking in the quarry communities of Tyndall and Garson. This is the source of Tyndall stone — the fossil-mottled limestone quarried here that clads the Manitoba Legislature and Canada's Parliament — so shallow bedrock is a defining local feature. Mixed farming, rural-residential acreages, and the quarry industry, along with the adjacent Brokenhead Ojibway Nation, shape the district.
