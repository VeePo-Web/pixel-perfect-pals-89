St. Malo is a francophone community of about 1,227 people in the Rural Municipality of De Salaberry, on the Rat River in southeastern Manitoba, best known for St. Malo Provincial Park with its reservoir, beach, and grotto. A recreation destination as much as a residential community, it blends year-round village homes with seasonal and lakeside property around the reservoir. Its French-Canadian heritage, its park-and-reservoir setting, and its rural character define its market.
