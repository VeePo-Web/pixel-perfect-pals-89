## About Headingley, Manitoba

Headingley is a rural municipality on Winnipeg's western boundary, spread along the Assiniboine River and split by the Trans-Canada Highway. Its 2021 population was 4,331, up 21% from 3,579 in 2016 — among the fastest growth rates in the Winnipeg Metro area. Incorporated in 1993 after separating from the City of Winnipeg, it covers 107.53 km² of estate lots, acreages and farmland, with a commercial strip along Portage Avenue West and the provincial Headingley Correctional Centre among its largest institutions.
