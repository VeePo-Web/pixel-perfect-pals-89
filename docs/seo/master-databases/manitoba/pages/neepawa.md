## About Neepawa, Manitoba

Neepawa is a town of 5,685 in western Manitoba, 170 km west of Winnipeg and 55 km northeast of Brandon at the junction of the Yellowhead Highway 16 and Highway 5. It grew 23.3% between 2016 and 2021, one of the fastest rates of any Manitoba town, driven largely by the HyLife pork-processing plant. Neepawa is the hometown of novelist Margaret Laurence and calls itself the Lily Capital of the World. The Whitemud River runs through town, and the Riding Mountain escarpment rises to the north.
