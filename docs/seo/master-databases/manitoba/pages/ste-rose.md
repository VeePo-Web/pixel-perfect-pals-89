## About Ste. Rose, Manitoba

Ste. Rose is a 630-square-kilometre municipality of 1,591 people (2021 census) in Manitoba's Parkland region, centred on the francophone-rooted town of Ste. Rose du Lac on the Turtle River. Known as the Cattle Capital of Manitoba for its auction mart, it is ranch and mixed-farm country on Highway 5, with cottage properties toward the Lake Manitoba shore. Population fell 7.1% from 1,712 in 2016. It sits 203 km northwest of Winnipeg and 133 km northeast of Brandon.
