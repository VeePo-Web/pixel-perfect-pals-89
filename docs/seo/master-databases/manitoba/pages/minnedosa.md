## About Minnedosa, Manitoba

Minnedosa is a town of 2,741 people (2021 census) in Manitoba's Westman region, 45 km north of Brandon on Highway 10. Terraced into the valley of the Little Saskatchewan River, it centres on Minnedosa Lake — a reservoir and recreation lake — and grew 11.9% between 2016 and 2021. A valley town on rolling till country toward the Riding Mountain uplands, its river-valley setting, its slope terrain, and its steady growth define its residential character.
