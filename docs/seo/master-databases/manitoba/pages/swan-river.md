## About Swan River, Manitoba

Swan River is a town of 4,049 people (2021 census) in Manitoba's Parkland region, 379 km northwest of Winnipeg. It anchors the Swan Valley, a broad agricultural basin tucked between the Duck Mountain and Porcupine Mountain uplands near the Saskatchewan border. Grain and cattle farming plus forestry drive the local economy, and the town serves as the retail, healthcare, and service centre for the whole valley. Its setting between two forested highlands brings heavier snowfall than the open prairie to the south.
