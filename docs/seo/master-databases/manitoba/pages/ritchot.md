## About Ritchot, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Ritchot is a fast-growing, largely francophone municipality of 7,469 people (2021 census) on the Red River just south of Winnipeg, taking in the ring-diked communities of St. Adolphe and Île-des-Chênes plus Grande Pointe and Ste. Agathe. Sitting on the Red River Valley floodplain and its expansive gumbo clay, it lives with the flooding the valley is known for — St. Adolphe's ring dike held through the 1997 Flood of the Century — making flood-aware property care a defining local concern.
