## About Gimli, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Gimli is a Lake Winnipeg municipality of 6,569 people (2021 census) on the lake's west shore, 86 km north of Winnipeg — the historic heart of New Iceland, the largest Icelandic settlement outside Iceland. It pairs a fishing-and-harbour town with a strong cottage economy, the Diageo distillery that ages Crown Royal, and the former airfield famous for the 1983 Gimli Glider landing. Its Icelandic heritage, its Lake Winnipeg shore, and its cottage-and-town mix define its market.
