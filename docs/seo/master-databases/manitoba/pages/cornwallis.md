## About Cornwallis, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Cornwallis wraps around the south and east sides of Brandon in southwestern Manitoba, covering 500.51 square kilometres with 4,568 residents at the 2021 census, up 1.4% from 2016. It includes the communities of Sprucewoods and Chater and borders CFB Shilo to the east. The Brandon Hills rise in its southern reaches, and much of the municipality is grain farmland mixed with a growing belt of country-residential acreages minutes from the city. Its administrative centre sits just 9 km from Brandon.
