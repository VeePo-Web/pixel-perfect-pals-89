## About Rossburn, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Rossburn is a Parkland municipality of 973 people (2021 census) on the western edge of the Riding Mountain uplands, 113 km northwest of Brandon. It sits near the Asessippi valley and Lake of the Prairies — the long reservoir on the Assiniboine River near the Saskatchewan border — an area known for the Asessippi ski hill and cottage country. Grain and cattle farming, Ukrainian and First Nations settlement heritage, and seasonal recreation shape the district.
