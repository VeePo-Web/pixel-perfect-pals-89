## About Victoria Beach, Manitoba

Victoria Beach is a Lake Winnipeg cottage municipality of 689 permanent residents (2021 census) on the lake's southeast shore, 93 km northeast of Winnipeg — though its summer population swells far higher. Overwhelmingly seasonal, it is famous for its restricted, car-free village area, where vehicles are banned in summer and residents walk or cycle. Its lakeshore cottages, its seasonal character, and its unique car-free access rules define a residential market unlike anywhere else in the province.
