## About Alonsa, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Alonsa is a large, sparsely populated municipality of 1,210 people (2021 census) spread across roughly 3,006 square kilometres along the west shore of Lake Manitoba in the southern Parkland, 182 km northwest of Winnipeg. It is ranch and mixed-farm country with lakeshore properties, and much of its low-lying land lies in the zone devastated by the 2011 Lake Manitoba flood — the province's costliest overland flood — which makes water and drainage a defining local concern.
