## About Victoria, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Victoria is a Central Plains municipality of 1,188 people (2021 census) south of the Assiniboine River, centred on the town of Holland, 129 km southwest of Winnipeg and 78 km east of Brandon. It is grain and mixed-farm country on the plains near the eastern edge of the Spruce Woods country, worked through open-prairie wind and deep-frost winters. The municipality grew 4.9% between 2016 and 2021.
