## About Taché, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Taché is a commuter and farming municipality of 11,916 people (2021 census) just east of Winnipeg, taking in the francophone community of Lorette and the growing community of Landmark along the Seine River corridor. Across roughly 581 square kilometres, it blends commuter subdivisions, rural-residential acreages, and mixed farmland. Its proximity to the city and its mix of French-heritage and new-build communities define a busy, growing residential market.
