## About Hanover, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Hanover is a fast-growing municipality of 17,216 people (2021 census) surrounding the City of Steinbach in southeastern Manitoba. One of the province's largest and most populous rural municipalities, it takes in the communities of Mitchell, Blumenort, Grunthal, Kleefeld, and New Bothwell — home of Bothwell Cheese — and is the heart of Manitoba's Mennonite dairy and agriculture belt. Its well-drained ground east of the Red River Valley and its steady growth define a busy mix of farm and new-build property.
