## About Mossey River, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Mossey River is a Parkland municipality of 1,450 people (2021 census) on the east side of Lake Winnipegosis, 282 km northwest of Winnipeg. It takes in the fishing town of Winnipegosis and the community of Fork River, and its economy mixes commercial lake fishing, grain and cattle farming, and lakeshore property. Its population grew sharply — 26.6% — between 2016 and 2021, one of the larger increases among Manitoba's rural municipalities.
