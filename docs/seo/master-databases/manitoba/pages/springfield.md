## About Springfield, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Springfield is a fast-growing municipality of 16,142 people (2021 census) on Winnipeg's eastern boundary, taking in Oakbank, Dugald, Anola, and Cooks Creek across roughly 1,096 square kilometres. A mix of commuter subdivisions, rural-residential acreages, and industrial parks along the city edge, it is one of Manitoba's larger rural municipalities. Its Cooks Creek area preserves notable Ukrainian heritage, and its position east of the Red River gives it more varied, generally better-drained ground than the city's clay core.
