## About Argyle, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Argyle is a small municipality of 994 people (2021 census) in the southwestern hill country of Manitoba, 164 km southwest of Winnipeg but just 75 km from Brandon. It takes in the communities of Baldur and Belmont, set on the rolling till ground at the western edge of the Pembina Valley near the Westman line. Grain and mixed-farm country with slope drainage and till soils, its hill-country setting, its farm mix, and its Brandon proximity define its market.
