## About Stanley, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Stanley is a large, prosperous municipality of 8,981 people (2021 census) that surrounds the cities of Winkler and Morden in Manitoba's Pembina Valley. One of the most productive farm districts in the province, it is the heart of the Mennonite West Reserve, blending intensive agriculture with rural-residential property on the Red River Valley clay below the Manitou Escarpment. Its farm economy, its Mennonite heritage, and its position around the twin cities define its market.
