## About Portage la Prairie, Manitoba

Portage la Prairie is a city of 13,270 (2021 census) on the Trans-Canada Highway, 83 km west of Winnipeg. It is Manitoba's agri-food processing capital: McCain Foods and J.R. Simplot run french-fry plants here, and Roquette operates one of the world's largest pea-protein plants. Crescent Lake, an oxbow of the Assiniboine River, curls around Island Park in the middle of the city. Southport, a former air force base just south, now operates as an aerospace and training campus. The surrounding Portage plains hold deep, stone-free lacustrine loam — some of Canada's richest soil.
