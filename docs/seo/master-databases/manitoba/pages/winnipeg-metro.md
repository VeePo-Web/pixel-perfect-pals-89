## About Winnipeg Metro

The Winnipeg Metro Region is Manitoba's population and economic core, centred on the City of Winnipeg (2021 census population 749,607 — more than half the province) at the meeting of the Red and Assiniboine rivers. The metro's flat Lake Agassiz clay plain carries the capital's aerospace, transportation-logistics (CentrePort Canada), finance and healthcare economy, plus the estate-lot community of Headingley on its western edge. Housing runs from pre-1920 Exchange District-era stock to the master-planned suburbs of Waverley West and Sage Creek.

## Across the Winnipeg Metro region

The Winnipeg Metro Region concentrates Manitoba's property demand like nowhere else: 749,607 residents in Winnipeg proper plus the fast-growing estate community of Headingley, all on the table-flat bed of glacial Lake Agassiz where the Red and Assiniboine rivers meet.
