## About Osborne Village, Manitoba

Osborne Village is Winnipeg's densest neighbourhood, home to about 12,745 people packed into a compact area just south of downtown across the Assiniboine River. Centred on Osborne Street, it is a walkable district of heritage walk-up apartments, converted older homes, and newer condominiums, long known as a hub of the city's arts, dining, and nightlife. Its mix of high density and older, close-set buildings gives it a character unlike anywhere else in Winnipeg.
