## About Rhineland, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Rhineland is a Pembina Valley municipality of 5,819 people (2021 census) in the heart of the Mennonite West Reserve, surrounding the town of Altona. Sunflower and grain country on the Red River Valley floor, it takes in Plum Coulee, Gretna, and the historic housebarn village of Neubergthal — the best-preserved Mennonite street village in North America. Its intensive farm economy, its Mennonite heritage, and its deep valley-floor clay define its market.
