## About Stony Mountain, Manitoba

Stony Mountain is a community of about 2,160 people in the Rural Municipality of Rockwood, just 22 km north of Winnipeg. It takes its name from a low limestone rise on the flat Interlake plain, and shallow bedrock is a defining local feature. Best known for Stony Mountain Institution, the federal penitentiary in operation since 1877, it is also a growing commuter community. Its limestone ground, its metro proximity, and its commuter character define its residential market.
