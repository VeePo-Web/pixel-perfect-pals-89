## About Dauphin, Manitoba

Dauphin is a city of 8,368 people (2021 census) in Manitoba's Parkland region, 249 km northwest of Winnipeg and 145 km north of Brandon. It occupies 12.67 square kilometres in the broad valley between Riding Mountain National Park to the south and Duck Mountain Provincial Park to the north, with the Vermillion River flowing through town. A CN railway town with an early-1900s core and roundhouse heritage, Dauphin is the Parkland's centre for healthcare, government, and agriculture, and hosts Dauphin's Countryfest and Canada's National Ukrainian Festival.
