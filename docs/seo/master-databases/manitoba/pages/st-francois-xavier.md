## About St. François Xavier, Manitoba

St. François Xavier is a rural municipality of 1,449 people (2021 census) on the Assiniboine River just west of Winnipeg, 38 km from the city on the Trans-Canada Highway. Known as the White Horse Plain and marked by the White Horse statue beside the highway, it carries deep Métis and francophone river-lot settlement heritage. The narrow river-lot properties running back from the Assiniboine, and the community's easy proximity to the metro, define its residential and acreage mix.
