## About Dauphin, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Dauphin is a 1,512.79-square-kilometre municipality of 2,136 people (2021 census, down 7% from 2,298 in 2016) that surrounds the City of Dauphin in Manitoba's Parkland region. Its territory runs from Riding Mountain National Park's north side to the shore of Dauphin Lake, crossed by the Vermillion River. The economy is grain and livestock farming, with lakeshore cottage communities on Dauphin Lake. It lies 252 km northwest of Winnipeg and 150 km north of Brandon.
