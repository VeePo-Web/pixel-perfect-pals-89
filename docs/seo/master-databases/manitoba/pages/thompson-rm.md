The Rural Municipality of Thompson is a Pembina Valley municipality of 1,518 people (2021 census) in south-central Manitoba, 102 km southwest of Winnipeg, centred on the heritage community of Miami at the toe of the Manitou Escarpment. Grain and mixed-farm country where the Red River Valley floor meets the rising escarpment, its farm economy, its escarpment-toe setting, and its clay-and-slope ground define its market. It is distinct from the northern City of Thompson.
