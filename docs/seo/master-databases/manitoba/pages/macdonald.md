## About Macdonald, Manitoba

The Rural Municipality of Macdonald covers 1,156 square kilometres of Red River Valley farmland on Winnipeg's southwest edge. Its population reached 8,120 in the 2021 census, up 13.4 per cent from 7,162 in 2016 — among Manitoba's fastest growth rates. Oak Bluff, at the Perimeter Highway, and La Salle, on the La Salle River, are its largest communities, with Sanford and Starbuck farther west. The RM mixes commuter estate subdivisions with grain farms on expansive gumbo clay, and the La Salle River winds through its eastern side.
