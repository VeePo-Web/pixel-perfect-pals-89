What is Toronto?

Toronto is the capital of Ontario and the largest city in Canada, with a 2021 census population of 2,794,356 on the north shore of Lake Ontario. It is the financial centre of Canada, home to Bay Street and the Toronto Stock Exchange, plus major tech, film, healthcare and university sectors (University of Toronto, Toronto Metropolitan, York). The most multicultural city in the world, it spans 158 neighbourhoods from downtown towers to postwar Scarborough, Etobicoke and North York.
