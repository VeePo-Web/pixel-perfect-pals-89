## About Shippagan, New Brunswick

Shippagan is an Acadian port town of 2,672 people (2021 census) at the northeastern tip of the Acadian Peninsula, Gloucester County, New Brunswick. It is the centre of Canada's peat-moss industry and a major snow-crab fishing port, home to the New Brunswick Aquarium and Marine Centre and a Université de Moncton campus. Set on a low point facing Lamèque Island, it grew 3.6 percent from 2016 to 2021 and sits 17 km from Caraquet.
