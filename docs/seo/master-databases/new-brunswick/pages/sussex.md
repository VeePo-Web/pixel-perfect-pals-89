## About Sussex, New Brunswick

Sussex is a town of 4,440 (2021 census) in the broad dairy-farming valley of the upper Kennebecasis River, in Kings County. Dozens of outdoor murals have earned it the title of Atlantic Canada's mural capital, and the Atlantic Balloon Fiesta fills its skies with hot-air balloons each fall. Covered bridges dot the surrounding valley. The town sits 70 km from Moncton and 66 km from Saint John, grew 3.7% from 2016 to 2021, and packs 2,219 dwellings into just 8.9 km².
