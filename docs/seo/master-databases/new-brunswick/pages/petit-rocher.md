## About Petit-Rocher, New Brunswick

Petit-Rocher is an Acadian fishing village of 1,954 people (2021 census) on the Chaleur Bay shore of Gloucester County, New Brunswick, between the city of Bathurst and the community of Belledune. Built around a working wharf and beach, it carries a local history tied to the region's zinc mining, interpreted at community sites. With a dense shore-terrace core at 432 people per square kilometre, it grew 3 percent from 2016 to 2021 and sits 19 km from Bathurst.
