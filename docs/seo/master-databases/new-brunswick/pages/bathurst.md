## About Bathurst, New Brunswick

Bathurst is a city of 12,157 people (2021 census) in Gloucester County, the regional service centre for northern New Brunswick. It sits on a sheltered harbour where the Nepisiguit River meets Chaleur Bay, with Youghall Beach on the harbour and the Daly Point Nature Reserve salt marsh nearby. Home to the K.C. Irving Regional Centre — where the QMJHL's Acadie-Bathurst Titan play — and a CCNB college campus, the city has been diversifying since the Brunswick zinc mine closed in 2013.
