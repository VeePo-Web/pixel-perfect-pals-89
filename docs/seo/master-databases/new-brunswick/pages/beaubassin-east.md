## About Beaubassin East / Beaubassin-est, New Brunswick

Beaubassin East / Beaubassin-est is a rural community of 6,718 people (2021 census) in Westmorland County, New Brunswick, stretching along the Northumberland Strait shore between Shediac and Cap-Pelé. It includes the communities of Grand-Barachois and Haute-Aboujagane. Spread across 291.02 km² of barrier-beach and salt-marsh coast at just 23.1 people per km², it grew 5.4 percent from 2016 — and 15.2 percent of its 3,368 dwellings are seasonal or vacant, classic cottage-country numbers.
