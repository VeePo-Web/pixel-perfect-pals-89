## About Lamèque, New Brunswick

Lamèque is an Acadian island town of 1,301 people (2021 census) on Lamèque Island, Gloucester County, New Brunswick, connected by causeway and bridge through Shippagan. Surrounded by commercial peat bogs and the waters of Chaleur Bay and the Gulf of St. Lawrence, it hosts the International Baroque Music Festival each summer — an internationally known event improbably rooted in this small island community. Lamèque sits 11 km from Shippagan and 25 km from Caraquet.
