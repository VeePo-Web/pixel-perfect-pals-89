## About Petitcodiac, New Brunswick

Petitcodiac is a village of 1,476 people in Westmorland County, New Brunswick, at the head of the Petitcodiac River valley on Route 106. It grew up as a railway junction and is now a farm-service village within commuting range of both Moncton, 34 km east, and Sussex, 35 km west. The village added 6.7% to its population between 2016 and 2021 and spreads 1,476 residents across 17.18 km².
