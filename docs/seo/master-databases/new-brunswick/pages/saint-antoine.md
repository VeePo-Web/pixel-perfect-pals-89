## About Saint-Antoine, New Brunswick

Saint-Antoine is a strongly francophone village of 1,791 people in inland Kent County, New Brunswick, 30 km from Moncton and within its commuting orbit. It is the birthplace of Louis J. Robichaud, New Brunswick's first elected Acadian premier. One of the most compact communities in the region, it packs its population into 6.32 km² at 283.6 people per km², and grew 3.3% between 2016 and 2021.
