## About Beresford, New Brunswick

Beresford is a francophone town of 4,294 people (2021 census) on the Chaleur Bay shore of Gloucester County, New Brunswick, just north of the city of Bathurst. Known for Beresford Beach and its protected saltmarsh, it functions largely as a residential and cottage extension of the Bathurst area. With a dense, settled core at 223 people per square kilometre, it grew marginally from 2016 to 2021 and sits only 11 km from downtown Bathurst.
