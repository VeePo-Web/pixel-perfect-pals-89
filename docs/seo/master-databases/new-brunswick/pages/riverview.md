## About Riverview, New Brunswick

Riverview is a town of 20,584 people (2021 census) in Albert County, New Brunswick, directly across the tidal Petitcodiac River from Moncton. The Gunningsville Bridge, opened in 2005, links the two downtowns just 5 km apart. Riverview grew 4.7 percent from 2016, packs 603.7 people per square kilometre into 34.1 km², and only 1.7 percent of its 8,797 dwellings sit vacant or seasonal. Marsh trails line its riverfront, and Mill Creek Nature Park anchors its green space.
