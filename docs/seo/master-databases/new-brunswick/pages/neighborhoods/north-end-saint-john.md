## About the North End of Saint John, New Brunswick

The North End is a historic working district of Saint John — Canada's first incorporated city (2021 census population 69,895) — lying north of the uptown peninsula in Saint John County, New Brunswick. Its older, largely pre-war housing runs along Main Street and the harbour-side streets. The district was physically divided from uptown when the Saint John Throughway was built through it, and it overlooks the harbour and the approaches to the Reversing Falls.

Its story turned on one piece of infrastructure: when the Saint John Throughway was pushed through in the highway era, it split the North End from uptown and reshaped the district for good.
