## About the West Side of Saint John, New Brunswick

The West Side is the portion of Saint John — Canada's first incorporated city (2021 census population 69,895) — lying west of the harbour in Saint John County, New Brunswick. It comprises the historic Carleton and Lancaster communities, and the Reversing Falls Rapids, where the Saint John River meets the Bay of Fundy, sit at its edge.
