## About Downtown Fredericton, New Brunswick

Downtown Fredericton is the historic core of the capital of New Brunswick (Fredericton's 2021 census population is 63,116), on the Saint John River in York County. It is built around the historic Garrison District and Officers' Square, with Queen Street as its heritage commercial spine and the Beaverbrook Art Gallery, the provincial legislature, and Christ Church Cathedral among its landmarks. The riverfront blocks carry real flood-zone awareness after the major Saint John River floods of 2018 and 2019.
