## About Uptown Saint John, New Brunswick

Uptown Saint John is the historic commercial and residential core of Saint John — Canada's first incorporated city (2021 census population 69,895) — set on the harbour hill of Saint John County, New Brunswick. Much of it was rebuilt in brick and stone after the Great Fire of 1877 destroyed the old wooden town, making it one of Canada's best-preserved 19th-century commercial districts. King Street and Prince William Street anchor its heritage retail core, and the Saint John City Market — Canada's oldest continuing farmers' market — sits at its heart, near King's Square and the Imperial Theatre.
