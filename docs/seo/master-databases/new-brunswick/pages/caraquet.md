## About Caraquet, New Brunswick

Caraquet is an Acadian town of 4,285 people (2021 census) on Chaleur Bay, Gloucester County, New Brunswick, widely regarded as the cultural capital of Acadie. Its Festival acadien, running since 1963, climaxes each August 15 in the Tintamarre, and the Village Historique Acadien living-history site sits just west of town. An active fishing port strung along a harbour terrace, Caraquet is 28 km from Tracadie and forms, with the surrounding Peninsula, the francophone heart of the province.
