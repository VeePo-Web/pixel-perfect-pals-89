## About the Fundy & Greater Saint John

The Fundy & Greater Saint John region spans Saint John, Kings, and Charlotte counties along the Bay of Fundy, home to the highest tides on Earth. Saint John is the province's industrial anchor — the Irving Oil refinery is Canada's largest, and Port Saint John handles container and cruise traffic — while the Kennebecasis Valley towns form its commuter belt and Charlotte County runs on the Ganong chocolate legacy, aquaculture, and resort tourism. Landmarks include the Reversing Falls Rapids, the Saint John City Market (Canada's oldest continuing farmers' market), the Algonquin Resort at St. Andrews, and the Swallowtail Lighthouse on Grand Manan.

## Communities we serve in the Fundy & Greater Saint John

- [Saint John](/areas/saint-john) — Canada's first incorporated city (69,895 people), the province's industrial anchor on the Bay of Fundy.
- [Quispamsis](/areas/quispamsis) — the Kennebecasis Valley's largest commuter town, 18,768 people northeast of Saint John.
- [Rothesay](/areas/rothesay) — a riverfront Kennebecasis town of 11,977 with historic estates and a common.
- [Grand Bay-Westfield](/areas/grand-bay-westfield) — a river-mouth commuter town of 4,967 where the Saint John opens into Grand Bay.
- [Hampton](/areas/hampton) — the Kings County shiretown (4,395 people) on the Kennebecasis marshes.
- [Sussex](/areas/sussex) — Atlantic Canada's mural capital, a dairy-valley town of 4,440.
- [St. Stephen](/areas/st-stephen) — Canada's Chocolate Town (4,510 people), home of Ganong Bros. on the St. Croix border.
- [Saint Andrews](/areas/saint-andrews) — Canada's original seaside resort town, 2,048 people on Passamaquoddy Bay.
- [St. George](/areas/st-george) — the Granite Town (1,579 people), on the Magaguadavic River gorge.
- [Grand Manan](/areas/grand-manan) — the big Fundy island (2,595 people), reached by a 90-minute ferry.
- [Norton](/areas/norton) — a Kennebecasis farm village of 1,410 between Sussex and Hampton.
- [Sussex Corner](/areas/sussex-corner) — a village of 1,458 at Sussex's shoulder in covered-bridge country.
- [Campobello Island](/areas/campobello-island) — FDR's beloved island (949 people), with year-round road access through Maine.
- [Blacks Harbour](/areas/blacks-harbour) — the sardine village (907 people) and mainland terminal for the Grand Manan ferry.
