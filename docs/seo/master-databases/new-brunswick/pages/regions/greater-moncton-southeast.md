## About the Greater Moncton & Southeast

The Greater Moncton & Southeast is the fastest-growing urban region in Atlantic Canada, spread across Westmorland, Albert, and Kent counties. Greater Moncton is the Maritimes' transportation, logistics, retail, and back-office hub, with Université de Moncton and two regional hospitals anchoring a bilingual workforce, while Kent County and the Strait shore run on fisheries, tourism, and Acadian small business. The region reaches from the tidal red-mud rivers off the Bay of Fundy to the warm Northumberland Strait, with landmarks from Hopewell Rocks and the Petitcodiac tidal bore to Parlee Beach and Kouchibouguac National Park.

## Communities we serve in the Greater Moncton & Southeast

- [Moncton](/areas/moncton) — Canada's first bilingual city (79,470 people), the Maritimes' logistics hub on the tidal Petitcodiac.
- [Dieppe](/areas/dieppe) — the fast-growing francophone-majority city of 28,114, home of the Greater Moncton airport.
- [Riverview](/areas/riverview) — a town of 20,584 across the Petitcodiac from Moncton by the Gunningsville Bridge.
- [Shediac](/areas/shediac) — the Lobster Capital of the World (7,535 people), where Parlee Beach makes summer the biggest industry.
- [Beaubassin East](/areas/beaubassin-east) — an Acadian rural community of 6,718 along the Strait cottage shore.
- [Sackville](/areas/sackville) — a Mount Allison University town of 6,099 on the Tantramar marshes.
- [Memramcook](/areas/memramcook) — the cradle of the Acadian renaissance, a valley village of 5,029.
- [Cocagne](/areas/cocagne) — an Acadian harbour community of 2,757 on the Cocagne River estuary.
- [Bouctouche](/areas/bouctouche) — K.C. Irving's hometown (2,513 people), behind the great dune on Bouctouche Bay.
- [Salisbury](/areas/salisbury) — a commuter village of 2,387 at the western gate of Greater Moncton.
- [Richibucto](/areas/richibucto) — the Kent County shiretown (1,411 people), a fishing port near Kouchibouguac.
- [Cap-Pelé](/areas/cap-pele) — a herring-smokehouse village of 2,441 on the warm Strait shore.
- [Saint-Antoine](/areas/saint-antoine) — the birthplace of premier Louis J. Robichaud, a growing Acadian village of 1,791.
- [Hillsborough](/areas/hillsborough) — a gypsum-country village of 1,348 near Hopewell Rocks.
- [Petitcodiac](/areas/petitcodiac) — a railway-junction village of 1,476 at the head of the Petitcodiac valley.
- [Dorchester](/areas/dorchester) — the sandstone shiretown of Westmorland County, 906 people over the Memramcook marshes.
- [Rexton](/areas/rexton) — the birthplace of UK prime minister Bonar Law, a village of 874 on the tidal Richibucto.
- [Saint-Louis de Kent](/areas/saint-louis-de-kent) — home of the Acadian flag's designer, a village of 981 by Kouchibouguac.
