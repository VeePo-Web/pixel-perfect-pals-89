## About Tracy, New Brunswick

Tracy is a village of 610 people (2021 census) in Sunbury County, New Brunswick, on the South Branch Oromocto River adjacent to Fredericton Junction. It lies within Fredericton's census metropolitan area, about 30 km south of the capital, making it a rural commuter village in the woods. Tracy covers 29.44 km² with 258 private dwellings — 97% of them occupied year-round.
