## About Richibucto, New Brunswick

Richibucto is a town of 1,411 people in Kent County, New Brunswick, set at the mouth of the Richibucto River 66 km north of Moncton. The historic shiretown of Kent County, it remains a working fishing port on the river's estuary and serves as a gateway to Kouchibouguac National Park. The town grew a strong 11.5% between 2016 and 2021, one of the healthier growth rates among small Kent County communities.
