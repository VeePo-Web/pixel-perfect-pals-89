## About Campobello Island, New Brunswick

Campobello Island is a rural community of 949 people (2021 census) in the Passamaquoddy islands of Charlotte County, New Brunswick. Roosevelt Campobello International Park preserves FDR's summer home at one end of the island, and Head Harbour Lightstation stands at the other. The only year-round road access is the FDR Memorial Bridge to Lubec, Maine. A fishing community spanning 39.59 km², Campobello grew 8.8% between 2016 and 2021, and nearly a third of its 612 dwellings are seasonal.
