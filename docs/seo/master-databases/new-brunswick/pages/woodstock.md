## About Woodstock, New Brunswick

Woodstock is New Brunswick's first incorporated town (1856) and the shiretown of Carleton County, with a historic court house. Home to 5,553 people at the 2021 census — up 6.2% from 2016 — it sits where the Meduxnekeag River meets the Saint John, at the junction of the Trans-Canada Highway and Route 95 to Houlton, Maine. Covering 14.96 km² at 371.1 people per km², Woodstock is the service centre for the upper Saint John River potato belt.
