## About Fredericton Junction, New Brunswick

Fredericton Junction is a village of 719 people (2021 census) in Sunbury County, New Brunswick, on the South Branch Oromocto River. It grew up as a railway junction on the Saint John–Fredericton line and now sits within commuting range of both Fredericton (32 km) and Oromocto (23 km). The village counts 304 private dwellings across 23.85 km², with 96% of them occupied year-round.
