## About Hanwell, New Brunswick

Hanwell is a rural community in York County, New Brunswick, incorporated in 2014 and strung along the Hanwell Road (Route 640) corridor southwest of Fredericton. Home to 4,743 people at the 2021 census, it is one of the capital region's fastest-growing communities. Hanwell covers a large 152.06 km² of wooded upland at a low density of 31.2 people per km², with 1,887 dwellings. Fredericton is 15 km away, and the community lies within the Fredericton census metropolitan area.
