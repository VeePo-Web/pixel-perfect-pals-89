## About Minto, New Brunswick

Minto is a village of 2,234 people (2021 census) in Queens County, New Brunswick, sitting near Grand Lake, the province's largest lake. Coal was mined in the Minto–Grand Lake field for centuries — among the earliest coal workings in North America — and since the mines and the power station closed, the local economy has leaned on forestry and commuting. The village covers 31.36 km² at 71.2 people per km², with 1,145 dwellings, about 8.9% of them seasonal or vacant. Fredericton is 48 km away.
