## About Neguac, New Brunswick

Neguac is an Acadian fishing village of 1,692 people (2021 census) on the sheltered inner shore of Miramichi Bay, in Northumberland County, New Brunswick. It keeps a working wharf and a beach on the inner bay, and serves as the northern gateway between the Miramichi and the Acadian Peninsula. The village covers 26.72 square kilometres, sits 24 km from the city of Miramichi, and grew 0.5% between 2016 and 2021.
