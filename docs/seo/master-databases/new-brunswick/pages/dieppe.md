## About Dieppe, New Brunswick

Dieppe is a city of 28,114 people (2021 census) in Westmorland County, New Brunswick, 7 km from Moncton. It grew 10.8 percent between 2016 and 2021, making it the province's fastest-growing major city, and it is New Brunswick's largest francophone-majority municipality. Dieppe hosts the Greater Moncton Roméo LeBlanc International Airport and CF Champlain (Champlain Place), the region's largest shopping centre. The city took its name in honour of the 1942 Dieppe Raid.
