## About Gagetown, New Brunswick

Gagetown is a village of 787 people (2021 census) on the Saint John River in Queens County, New Brunswick, and the county's historic shiretown. It is known for artisan studios, a heritage riverfront, and the Gagetown–Jemseg cable ferry crossing — and it is distinct from CFB Gagetown, which is located at Oromocto. The village grew 10.7% between 2016 and 2021 and counts 341 private dwellings across 49.32 km².
