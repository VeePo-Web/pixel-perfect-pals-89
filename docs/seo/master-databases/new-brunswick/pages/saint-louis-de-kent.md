## About Saint-Louis de Kent, New Brunswick

Saint-Louis de Kent is an Acadian village of 981 people on Route 134 in northern Kent County, New Brunswick, and a gateway community for Kouchibouguac National Park. It is the home village of Father Marcel-François Richard, who designed the Acadian tricolour flag. One of the region's fastest-growing small communities, it gained 14.6% in population between 2016 and 2021 within a remarkably compact 1.98 km² footprint.
