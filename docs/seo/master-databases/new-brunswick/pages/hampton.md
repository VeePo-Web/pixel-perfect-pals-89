## About Hampton, New Brunswick

Hampton is the shiretown of Kings County, with 4,395 residents at the 2021 census — up 2.5% from 2016. It sits on a low marshland bend of the Kennebecasis River, 33 km from Saint John at the east end of the Kennebecasis Valley commuter belt. Hampton is the birthplace of John Peters Humphrey, principal drafter of the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights, honoured with a monument in town.
