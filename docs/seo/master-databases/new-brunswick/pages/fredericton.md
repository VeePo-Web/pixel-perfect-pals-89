## About Fredericton, New Brunswick

Fredericton is the capital of New Brunswick, home to 63,116 people at the 2021 census — up 7.5% since 2016. It sits on the Saint John River (Wolastoq) in York County, 88 km from Saint John. The city hosts the University of New Brunswick (founded 1785, among Canada's oldest universities) and St. Thomas University, the historic Garrison District and Officers' Square, the Beaverbrook Art Gallery, RCMP J Division headquarters, and a growing IT and cybersecurity sector. Odell Park preserves old-growth forest inside city limits.
