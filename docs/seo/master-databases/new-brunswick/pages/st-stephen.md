St. Stephen is a border town of 4,510 people (2021 census) in Charlotte County, New Brunswick, on the St. Croix River facing Calais, Maine. Known as Canada's Chocolate Town, it is the home of Ganong Bros., the country's oldest candy company, and hosts the Chocolate Museum and an annual Chocolate Fest.
