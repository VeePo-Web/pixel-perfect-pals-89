## About St. George, New Brunswick

St. George is a town of 1,579 people (2021 census) in Charlotte County, New Brunswick, known as the Granite Town for its red-granite quarrying history. The Magaguadavic River's falls and gorge cut right through the village, and Lake Utopia — famous for its lake-monster folklore — lies nearby. A service centre for Charlotte County's Fundy shore, St. George sits 61 km from Saint John and grew 4.1% between 2016 and 2021.
