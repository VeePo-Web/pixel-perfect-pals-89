## About Salisbury, New Brunswick

Salisbury is a village of 2,387 people in Westmorland County, New Brunswick, sitting 21 km west of Moncton where Route 112 meets the Trans-Canada Highway. Built on the upper reaches of the Petitcodiac River, it was a historic centre of early-1900s silver-fox fur farming and is now a commuter village inside the Moncton census metropolitan area, growing 4.5% between 2016 and 2021.
