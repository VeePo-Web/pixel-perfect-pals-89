## About Hillsborough, New Brunswick

Hillsborough is a village of 1,348 people in Albert County, New Brunswick, set on gypsum hills above the tidal Petitcodiac River, 21 km from Moncton. It hosts the New Brunswick Railway Museum and the Salem & Hillsborough heritage railroad, carries a long history of gypsum quarrying, and is the birthplace of William Steeves, a Father of Confederation. Hopewell Rocks on the Fundy shore is twenty minutes away.
