## About Rexton, New Brunswick

Rexton is a village of 874 people on the tidal Richibucto River in Kent County, New Brunswick, directly across from the town of Richibucto and 62 km north of Moncton. It is the birthplace of Andrew Bonar Law, UK prime minister in 1922–23 — the only British prime minister born outside the British Isles — and the Bonar Law historic site commemorates the family homestead.
