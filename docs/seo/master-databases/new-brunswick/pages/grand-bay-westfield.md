## About Grand Bay-Westfield, New Brunswick

Grand Bay-Westfield is a town of 4,967 (2021 census) in Kings County, where the Saint John River widens into Grand Bay about 17 km from Saint John. It was formed in 1998 by amalgamating the communities of Grand Bay and Westfield. Free river cable ferries connect the nearby Kingston Peninsula. A low-density commuter town, it spreads 2,031 dwellings across 59.82 km² of wooded shoreline — about 83 people per km².
