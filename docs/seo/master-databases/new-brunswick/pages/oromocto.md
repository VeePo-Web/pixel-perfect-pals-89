## About Oromocto, New Brunswick

Oromocto is a town of 9,045 people (2021 census) in Sunbury County, New Brunswick, sitting where the Oromocto River meets the Saint John. Purpose-built in the late 1950s and nicknamed "Canada's Model Town," it serves CFB Gagetown, Canada's largest military training base. The town covers 22.36 km² at a density of 404.5 people per km², counts 3,897 dwellings, and lies 19 km from Fredericton and 71 km from Saint John. Its large rotating military population shapes the local housing market.
