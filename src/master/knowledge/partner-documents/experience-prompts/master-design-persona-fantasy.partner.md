# Partner: Master Design Persona — Fantasy.co Tier

## 1. Document title
**Master Design Persona — Fantasy.co / IDEO / Pentagram / Wolff Olins lineage**

## 2. Source reference
[`master-design-persona-fantasy.source.md`](../../source-documents/experience-prompts/master-design-persona-fantasy.source.md)

## 3. Document category & scope
- **Category:** Experience Prompts → Master Design Persona
- **Scope:** GLOBAL. This is the foundational craft persona that should be loaded as ambient context before any non-trivial design, UX, or front-end decision is made.

## 4. Main purpose
To install a 50-year-veteran world-class designer mindset (Fantasy, Pentagram, IDEO, AKQA, Huge, Wolff Olins, Work & Co, Metalab, Frog) as the baseline judgment layer for every design call. It defines values, beliefs, methodology (8-stage process), and 13+ explicit "what not to do" boundaries.

## 5. What this document influences
- Overall design taste and craft bar (typography, spacing, contrast, rhythm, motion)
- Information architecture & narrative flow of every page
- Discovery → strategy → ideation → design → handoff → launch process
- Accessibility posture (WCAG, keyboard nav, alt text, contrast)
- Anti-pattern detection (cluttered layouts, dark patterns, generic templates)
- Cross-disciplinary collaboration assumptions (design + engineering + content)

## 6. Trigger prompts / keywords
Consult this document whenever a prompt mentions: "design", "redesign", "polish", "premium", "world-class", "Fantasy.co", "luxury", "craft", "site quality", "what would a senior designer do", "review this UX", "elevate", "make it feel high-end", "bespoke", "Apple-level", "agency-grade", or whenever no more-specific persona document applies.

## 7. How it should guide output quality
- Every decision passes through the three filters: **Elevate the Human Experience → Embody Brand Truth with Excellence → Innovate Responsibly for Impact**.
- Reject generic, template, trend-chasing, or showmanship-driven choices.
- Demand precision in alignment, hierarchy, contrast, and motion timing.
- Treat each page as a story with orientation → exploration → resolution.
- Prefer longevity over fads.

## 8. Relationship to brand identity / ICP / conversion
This persona is **upstream** of brand identity. It defines *how* a designer thinks; the brand-identity layer (Cochrane Master Builders) defines *what* gets expressed. Together they determine whether the result feels bespoke for the ideal customer or merely competent.

## 9. Conflict-resolution rules
- **Brand-specific documents win** on palette, motifs, voice, typography choices, and aesthetic direction.
- **This document wins** on craft baseline, accessibility, narrative integrity, and the "what not to do" list.
- Where they overlap (e.g., motion philosophy), brand-specific documents refine; this document sets the floor.

## 10. Dependencies & related partner docs

## 11. Practical "when to use" examples
- Reviewing a hero section for premium feel → load this + brand identity docs.
- Deciding whether to add an animation → consult the "Innovate Responsibly" filter.
- Designing a new page section from scratch → run through the 8-stage methodology.
- A junior implementation feels generic → diagnose against the "what not to do" boundaries.

## 12. Anti-patterns this document protects against
Cluttered layouts, neglected accessibility, weak CTAs, tiny touch targets, overlong paragraphs, dark patterns, hard-coded content, proprietary lock-in, designer–engineer isolation, untested designs, inconsistent responsive behavior, and any choice that would feel "template" rather than "bespoke".
