# Partner: Brand Identity Architect

## 1. Document title
**Brand Identity Architect — Landor & Fitch / Wolff Olins / Collateral Lineage**

## 2. Source reference
[`brand-identity-architect.source.md`](../../source-documents/brand-identity/brand-identity-architect.source.md)

## 3. Document category & scope
- **Category:** Brand Identity → Strategic Architect
- **Scope:** GLOBAL. The methodology spine for turning fragmented inputs (market research, business description, design preferences, ICP) into a single coherent identity system.

## 4. Main purpose
To install a senior brand identity consultant operating system — rigor, restraint, repeatability — that produces a "Brand Identity North Star" document with non-negotiable truths, 3–5 operational core values, defensible positioning, identity signals, customer mantra, story system, verbal & visual identity, and what-not-to-do guardrails.

## 5. What this document influences
- Strategy work: positioning, USP, mission/vision/values articulation
- Verbal identity: voice, tone, lexicon, taboo language, copy do/don'ts
- Visual identity direction: palette logic, typography logic, composition rules
- Customer-facing language ("If you are X, you are our people")
- Conflict resolution between competing inputs
- The structure and depth of any brand-identity-related deliverable

## 6. Trigger prompts / keywords
Consult this document for: "brand identity", "positioning", "mission", "vision", "USP", "tone of voice", "brand voice", "elevator pitch", "tagline", "brand story", "brand guidelines", "make this on-brand", "north star", "verbal identity", "brand truth", "ideal customer mirror", "differentiation", "premium positioning", "category", "competitive edge".

## 7. How it should guide output quality
- Discovery → Synthesis → Decision (never jump to visuals first).
- Never claim "premium" without defining the *mechanics of premium*.
- Differentiation must be perceptible in seconds across copy, layout, imagery, offer.
- Resolve every input conflict with the explicit hierarchy: **customer truth + category reality → founder truth → business constraints → aesthetics**.
- Output one direction, not options. Commit.

## 8. Relationship to brand identity / ICP / conversion
This is the **methodology** for brand identity itself. The brand-specific Cochrane Master Builders documents are the *output* of this kind of process and take precedence over any general claims here. This document tells the system *how to think* about identity; brand docs tell it *what the identity is*.

## 9. Conflict-resolution rules
- **Brand-specific documents always win** on actual identity decisions for Cochrane Master Builders.
- **This document wins** on the *process* used when extending or refining identity (e.g., adding a sub-brand, voice for a new audience, fresh positioning for a new offering).
- Visual preferences must never override customer truth or market reality.

## 10. Dependencies & related partner docs
- `colours-and-shapes-experience-philosophy.partner.md` — complementary philosophical lens (Goodness/Truth/Beauty).
- `master-design-persona-fantasy.partner.md` — craft execution on top of identity.
- All `partner-documents/brands/cochrane-master-builders/` files — the actual identity to defer to.

## 11. Practical "when to use" examples
- "Write a tagline" → run through positioning + customer mantra steps first.
- "Is this copy on-brand?" → check against verbal identity rules + taboo language.
- "Should we describe ourselves as premium?" → demand proof mechanics before agreeing.
- "What's our differentiator?" → use the differentiation-perceptible-in-seconds test.

## 12. Anti-patterns this document protects against
Generic branding, unproven claims, contradictions, aesthetic drift, "brand theater", founder cosplay, options-instead-of-decisions, premium claims with no mechanics, and visual preferences overriding customer truth.
