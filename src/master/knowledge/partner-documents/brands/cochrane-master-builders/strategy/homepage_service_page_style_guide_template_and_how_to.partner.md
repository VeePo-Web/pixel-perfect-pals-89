### 10-step bespoke-domain workflow (§6)

Service Emotion → Hidden Fear → Hidden Work → Service Palette Variation → Main Visual Object → Hero around Buying Trigger → Service-Specific Component Stack → Pricing around Real Drivers → Adapt Proof → Service-Specific CTA.

## Pass/Fail Auditor gates

### Homepage (§12 — 10 items)

- [ ] First screen explains master brand and why it matters.
- [ ] Family-owned standard visible or clearly implied.
- [ ] Service path findable in under 10 seconds.
- [ ] Pricing guidance visible before contact form.
- [ ] Process section reduces fear.
- [ ] Page feels premium through clarity, not decoration.
- [ ] Local areas visible without keyword stuffing.
- [ ] CTAs clear, low-pressure, above the fold on mobile.
- [ ] No fake claims or unsupported badges.
- [ ] Loads quickly and remains stable on mobile.

### Service page (§13 — 13 items)

- [ ] Exact service obvious in hero.
- [ ] Page explains the real customer fear.
- [ ] Page explains hidden work that affects durability.
- [ ] Lists specific services and price ranges.
- [ ] Pricing includes drivers + assumptions.
- [ ] Process includes service-specific steps.
- [ ] Page connects service to family-owned long-term value.
- [ ] Includes proof or honest proof-building framework.
- [ ] FAQ answers real buyer questions.
- [ ] CTA matches service and buying stage.
- [ ] No duplicated generic copy across domains.
- [ ] Page feels useful enough to deserve ranking.
