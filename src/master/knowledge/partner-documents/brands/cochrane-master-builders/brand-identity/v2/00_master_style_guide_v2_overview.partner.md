## Brand Spine summary (canonical lives in `01_…`)

- **Category stance:** A family-owned construction services network for Cochrane homes, acreages, and surrounding communities — not a contractor template, not a franchise.
- **Enemy / rejection:** Generic-template contractor sites; price-by-bait; vague "quality / value / solutions" copy; templated 115-link service flatness.
- **Audience identity:** Cochrane-area homeowners, acreage owners, and small-commercial operators who need to understand the work *before* they trust the team with it.
- **Promise:** Old-school accountability, modern-day clarity. Strong foundations for those who come after us.
- **Proof:** Family-owned origin; clear pricing guidance with drivers; specification-sheet visual language; local community names; honest proof-building framework when proof is incomplete.
- **Personality:** Plainspoken, calm, structured, locally rooted, premium-through-clarity.
- **Standards:** No fake urgency; no fabricated proof; pricing always shown as ranges with drivers; family-owned standard visible on every page; mobile-first; load fast or fail.
