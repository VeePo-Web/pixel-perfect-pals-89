- **Wins** on keyword choice, meta title/description, slug, schema selection, Areas-We-Serve seeds, AI-search visibility.
- Loses to `brand-identity/1.2.x` on the *wording* that wraps the keyword skeleton.

## 8. Rules — non-negotiables this doc enforces

1. **Every page has one primary keyword.** Multi-keyword targeting dilutes ranking; secondary keywords live in body, not title. (source: keyword cluster methodology)
2. **Meta titles ≤ 60 chars, meta descriptions ≤ 160 chars.** Over-length truncates in SERP. (industry baseline reinforced by source)
3. **No duplicate meta titles across the site.** Areas-We-Serve pages especially must vary by neighborhood + service combination, never `${service} in ${city}` boilerplate.
4. **One H1 per page.** Always. (crawl hygiene)
5. **JSON-LD per page type is mandatory, not optional.** LocalBusiness on home + contact, Service on each service page, FAQPage on FAQ blocks, BreadcrumbList everywhere ≥ depth 2. (source: structured-data section)
6. **Areas-We-Serve depth, not breadth.** Each area page includes neighborhoods, postal coverage, lat/lng, local trust signals (license valid in jurisdiction), and at least 200 words of locally-grounded copy. Listicle pages of 50 cities with 30 words each fail. (source: local-area section + cross-link to `gr-areas-we-serve-excellence`)
7. **AI-search visibility requires citation-ready phrasing.** Pages that want LLM citations include atomic, attribution-friendly sentences ("CMB has built X custom homes in Cochrane since YEAR" beats "we've built lots over the years"). (source: AI-SEO section)
8. **FAQ blocks are sourced from real SERP questions, not invented.** Use SERP "People Also Ask" mined in this doc; never fabricate FAQs. (source: SERP-intent section)
9. **Long-tail page exists only if intent + on-mission both pass.** SEO opportunity alone never justifies a page; route through `strategy/*` first.
10. **Competitor names appear in research, never in front-end copy.** Mention competitors only when the source explicitly authorizes a comparison page (rare).
11. **Internal links use descriptive anchors, never "click here."** Anchor text is keyword-rich but human. (crawl hygiene)
12. **Image alt text is descriptive of content, not stuffed.** "Custom home exterior in Bow Valley" beats "custom home Cochrane builder Alberta." (cross-link to `gr-modern-image-pipeline`)
13. **No keyword cannibalization.** Two pages targeting the same primary keyword require a consolidation decision before launch.
14. **Brand-voice override always wins on word choice.** Keyword *intent* governs structure; brand identity governs vocabulary.

## 9. Anti-patterns

- Meta title `Drywall in Cochrane | Cochrane Master Builders` repeated on 12 area pages with only city changed.
- An Areas-We-Serve listicle page covering 50 cities at 30 words each. Violates Rule 6.
- An FAQ block invented from "what the team thinks people might ask." Violates Rule 8.
- A long-tail page targeting a juicy keyword that has no on-mission justification. Violates Rule 9.
- Hero H1 "Best Quality Affordable Premium Custom Homes Cochrane Alberta Builder" — keyword stuffing + multi-target. Violates Rules 1 + 14.
- Image alt text `home builder cochrane drywall renovation contractor`. Violates Rule 12.
- "We're better than \[Competitor\]" copy on a service page. Violates Rule 10.
- Two service pages both targeting "custom home builder Cochrane" with no consolidation. Violates Rule 13.
