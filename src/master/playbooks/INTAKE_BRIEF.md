# INTAKE BRIEF — what to gather before remixing a sub-service site

> If you skip this phase, the rest of the checklist becomes guesswork. **No remix begins without these inputs.** Drop the docs into the chat and reference them by filename.

---

## 1. Trade master brief (1 doc, ≥1 page)

A single document covering, in order:

1. **Legal business name** (exact, including Inc./Ltd.)
2. **Trading name** (what customers call them)
3. **Kebab-case slug** (e.g. `roofing`, `drywall-insulation`) — must be unique across `src/master/trades.ts`
4. **Parent category** — one of:
   `interior-finishing | exterior | structural | mechanical | electrical | landscape | specialty`
5. **Founding story** (≥150 words — who started it, why, when, what changed)
6. **3–5 USPs** — each provable. "Family-owned since 2024" doesn't count without a story.
7. **Pricing model** — hourly | fixed | per-sqft | quote-based. Note quote turnaround.
8. **Scope of work** — every job type in plain English
9. **What they REFUSE to do** — explicit list. Sets boundaries in copy.
10. **Target customer profile** — homeowner age range, income band, neighborhood type
11. **5-year vision** — informs tone (scrappy challenger vs. established authority)

## 2. Service catalogue (spreadsheet)

| Column | Required | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Service name | yes | Roof replacement |
| Sub-service | no | Asphalt shingle replacement |
| Scope | yes | Full tear-off + re-shingle, single-storey |
| Includes | yes | Underlayment, ice-and-water shield, ridge venting |
| Excludes | yes | Decking repair (quoted separately) |
| Materials / brands | yes | GAF Timberline HDZ, IKO Cambridge |
| Typical timeline | yes | 1–2 days |
| Price band low / mid / high | yes | $8k / $14k / $22k |
| Seasonality | no | May–October peak |
| Prerequisites | no | Roof inspection report |

This drives Phase 4 service-page copy and Phase 6 keyword maps.

## 3. Service-area master spreadsheet

| Column | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Area name | yes | Cochrane, Springbank, Bearspaw, Sunset Ridge |
| Type | yes | city / town / hamlet / neighborhood |
| Priority rank | yes | 1 = primary, 2 = secondary, 3 = opportunistic |
| Drive-time from HQ | yes | minutes |
| Lat / Lng | preferred | decimal degrees |
| Notable landmarks | yes | for unique area-page intros |
| Notable neighborhoods | yes | for nested mentions |

**Every row gets its own page** in Phase 6. Without this list, you ship duplicate area pages and Google demotes the site.

## 4. Sister-site backlink map

From the master spreadsheet plan. Each row:

| Source site | Target site | Anchor variants (3+) | Placement |
|---|---|---|---|
| cochranedrywall.ca | cochraneroofing.ca | "Cochrane roofing", "trusted local roofers", "roof replacement Cochrane" | footer + body widget |

## 5. Compliance documents

- Provincial / municipal license number
- WCB (or workers' comp) number
- Liability insurance certificate (carrier name + policy #)
- Trade-specific certifications (e.g. RCABC, manufacturer-certified installer)
- Any required permit-process language

These render in the footer + about page (Phase 8) and feed E-E-A-T signals (Phase 6).

## 6. Founder bio + real photos of work

- **Bio**: ≥120 words. Years in trade, why they started, philosophy, fun fact.
- **Photos**: a folder of REAL completed work. **No faces. No people.** Master rule.
  Aim for ≥30 candidate shots — Phase 5 picks the gallery from this pool.

## 7. Competitor audit (3 best-in-class)

Pick 3 sites in this trade — local + national. For each:

- Full-page screenshot (mobile + desktop)
- What they do well (hero, trust, services, booking, copy tone)
- What they do poorly
- Anything to STEAL (ideas, not copy)
- Anything to AVOID

Sets the bar for Phase 3 IA and Phase 4 copy without copying.

## 8. Color & mood direction

- One accent HSL (overrides master only with documented reason)
- Hero mood: residential | industrial | luxury | utilitarian
- Photography palette: warm | cool | neutral

## 9. Pricing & quote policy

- Are quotes free?
- In-person required, or remote OK?
- Same-day callback target?
- Minimum job size?
- Travel charge outside primary area?

Drives Phase 4 microcopy + Phase 7 booking modal.

## 10. Warranty & guarantee terms

- Years covered
- What's covered
- What's NOT covered
- Claim process
- Transferability to next homeowner

Drives the Phase 8 warranty page.

## 11. Voice do's & don'ts

5 things this trade SHOULD say. 5 things it must NEVER say.
Specific phrases — not "be friendly". E.g. **DO**: "plumb-and-true". **DON'T**: "best in the business".

---

## Hand-off

When all 11 items are uploaded, mark Phase 1 complete on the checklist and begin Phase 2. **Do not start Phase 2 with anything missing — fabricated facts will surface in Phase 6 schema validation and Phase 8 legal copy.**
