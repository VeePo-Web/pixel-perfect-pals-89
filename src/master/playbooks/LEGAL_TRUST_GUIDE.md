# LEGAL & TRUST GUIDE

> A trade site without real legal pages, real numbers, and real trust signals reads as suspicious — and Google's E-E-A-T model agrees. **Phase 8 is not optional.**

> **Disclaimer**: This is operational guidance for the remix process, not legal advice. Have the final pages reviewed by counsel.

---

## Privacy policy (`/privacy`)

Must reflect what the site ACTUALLY does. Cover:

- **Who you are** — legal business name + contact
- **What you collect** — name, phone, email, message body, plus any analytics-collected IP/cookie data
- **Why you collect it** — to respond to inquiries, deliver quotes, send service updates
- **Who processes it** — Lovable Cloud (managed backend), email provider, analytics provider, any other 3rd parties
- **How long you keep it** — retention period for leads, customers, archived data
- **User rights** — access, correction, deletion (GDPR / PIPEDA / provincial privacy law)
- **How to request deletion** — email + response window
- **Cookies / tracking** — what runs and why
- **Updates policy** — how changes are communicated
- **Contact** — privacy@ inbox or owner email

**Don't** copy a generic template. **Don't** claim you don't collect data when forms exist.

## Terms of service (`/terms`)

Trade-specific. Cover:

- Scope of services offered
- Quotation process (validity period, what triggers re-quote)
- Change-order policy
- Payment terms (deposit %, milestone schedule, final payment)
- Warranty reference (link to `/warranty`)
- Liability limitations (within provincial law)
- Dispute resolution (governing province, arbitration if any)
- Cancellation policy
- Force majeure

## Warranty page (`/warranty`)

From Phase 1 warranty terms:

- **Years covered** (workmanship vs. materials separately)
- **What's covered** — itemized
- **What's NOT covered** — itemized (acts of god, abuse, modification, etc.)
- **Claim process** — step-by-step, response window
- **Transferability** — does it transfer to next homeowner?
- **Materials warranty** — link to manufacturer warranties if applicable

## Cookie notice

- **Skip if** the site uses zero non-essential cookies (no analytics, no marketing pixels)
- **Otherwise**: minimal banner, dismissible, links to `/privacy`. Don't block content.

## Accessibility statement (`/accessibility`)

Short page (~200 words) declaring:

- Target standard (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Known limitations (be honest)
- Contact for accessibility issues
- Last reviewed date

## Trust signals rendered site-wide

Every page footer must show:

- License # (real, formatted clearly)
- Insurance carrier + policy # (or "fully insured" with specifics on /about)
- WCB # (where applicable)
- Real physical address (or service-area + "by appointment" if no storefront)
- Real local phone (`tel:` link)
- Real business hours
- Year founded
- Agency credit (VeePo, per existing brand memory)

## NAP consistency (Name / Address / Phone)

Must be **byte-identical** across:

- Site footer
- Contact page
- Schema markup (LocalBusiness JSON-LD)
- Google Business Profile
- Yelp, BBB, HomeStars, and every directory listing

Local SEO penalizes drift — even "Suite 5" vs "#5" vs "Unit 5" hurts.

## E-E-A-T signal checklist (also Phase 6)

- License & insurance numbers visible (not buried in /terms)
- Founder bio + photo of WORK (not face) on /about
- Real testimonials with name + city (not stock)
- Years-in-business stated
- Photos of completed work in this trade's actual jurisdictions
- Warranty page exists and links from footer
- HTTPS everywhere, no mixed content
- Privacy + Terms pages linked from footer

---

## Sign-off

Phase 8 is signed off when:

1. Every page above exists, is unique to this trade, and is reviewed by the owner
2. Counsel has reviewed `/privacy` and `/terms` (or owner has accepted the risk in writing)
3. NAP audit shows zero drift across all surfaces
4. License / insurance / WCB numbers render in the footer and `/about`
