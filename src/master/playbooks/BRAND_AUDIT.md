# BRAND AUDIT — Does this remix feel like Cochrane Master Builders?

Walk the site top-to-bottom on mobile and desktop. For each question, the answer must be **yes**.

## Visual

- [ ] Body background is bone (warm fresh paint), never pure white
- [ ] Headlines are Cormorant Garamond; body is Inter
- [ ] One accent color total (no two competing hues)
- [ ] Borders/hairlines feel like drywall seams (subtle, never harsh)
- [ ] No bright red, neon orange, or saturated yellow anywhere
- [ ] No glossy black sections; charcoal only

## Voice

- [ ] Copy sounds calm, honest, local — never hype, never corporate
- [ ] No words from the master `wordsToAvoid` list (luxury, premium, world-class, etc.)
- [ ] No "we're #1" / "best in Cochrane" claims
- [ ] Pricing framed as ranges + "send a couple of photos", not opaque quotes

## Photography

- [ ] No faces, no people in any image
- [ ] All images look real, not illustrated or stock-y
- [ ] Lighting is controlled, residential, calm
- [ ] Before/afters tell the transformation story for *this* trade

## Information

- [ ] Hero answers "what do you do, where, for whom" in <5 seconds
- [ ] Trust bar lives above the fold on mobile
- [ ] FAQ addresses the actual fears for this trade (not generic)
- [ ] Footer lists ≥3 sister sites with anchor text

## Mechanics

- [ ] Booking form submits successfully
- [ ] Confirmation email arrives with the new trade name in the subject
- [ ] All `/areas/*` pages render with LocalBusiness schema
- [ ] Lighthouse mobile ≥ 90

If any item fails, fix it before deploying. The whole point of the master template is consistency across 100 sites.
