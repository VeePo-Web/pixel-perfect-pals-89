## Main Purpose
This document establishes the exact psychological profile and UX expectations for the **Primary Consumer Decision Maker: The Mother / Matriarch**. In the context of residential construction and remodeling, she is the ultimate gatekeeper of the home, prioritizing safety, cleanliness, communication, and high-end aesthetic trust. This persona forces the UI and copywriting to shift away from "rough construction talk" and instead project luxury, emotional security, and generational legacy.

## What This Document Influences
This document acts as an immediate backend decision filter for:
- **Consumer-Facing Services Pages:** How a remodel or build is pitched. The emphasis shifts to predictable timelines, daily cleanup guarantees, and vetted crews.
- **Visual Design & Cleanliness:** Dictates a ruthless standard for Apple-level UX cleanliness. The site must "feel" as mathematically clean and safe as they expect their home to be during the build.
- **Copywriting Tone:** Warm, deeply trustworthy, and fundamentally protective. It must assure the ICP that their family's peace of mind is the ultimate priority.
- **Sub-Brand Generation:** When expanding to child services (e.g. Cochrane Tile Masters), this persona dictates that the residential service copy addresses her specific anxieties (e.g. "Will this create dust in my kitchen?").
