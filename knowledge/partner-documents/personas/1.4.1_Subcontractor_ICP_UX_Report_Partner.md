## Main Purpose
This document establishes the exact psychological profile, friction points, and conversion triggers for the **Subcontractor** persona. It explicitly outlines how the website should behave, sound, and structure user flows when speaking B2B (Business to Business) to highly skilled tradespeople or potential subcontractor partners. 

## What This Document Influences
This document acts as an immediate backend decision filter for:
- **B2B Funnels & Landing Pages:** "Work With Us", "Careers", or Subcontractor Lead Generation forms.
- **Copywriting Tone for Trades:** The language shifted away from homeowner sales, focusing instead on mutual legacy, reliability, clear payment terms, and high-end professional standards. 
- **UX Form Design:** Dictates creating hyper-efficient, frictionless, and highly professional forms. Subcontractors don't want fluff; they want efficiency married with the `fantasy.co` premium feel.
- **Sub-Brand Generation:** When expanding to child services (e.g. Cochrane Tile Masters), this persona dictates how that specific trade attracts its own sub-contractor labor force.
