## Main Purpose
This document is the **Ultimate North Star** for the Master Builders brand identity. It dictates the absolute boundaries of what the brand IS, and crucially, what the brand IS NOT. It establishes the unshakeable philosophical direction of the company: a legacy-focused, deeply traditional, uncompromised luxury builder. It serves as the final arbiter for all creative, copy, and structural decisions.

## What This Document Influences
This document acts as an immediate backend decision filter for:
- **Global Brand Aura:** Forces all design logic and copywriting to project "timeless luxury" rather than "fast, cheap contractor." It guarantees the website does not degrade into a generic lead-gen trap.
- **Aesthetic Boundaries:** If there is ever a conflict between a flashy modern UX trend (e.g., hyper-minimalist brutalism) and the brand identity, this document commands the system to stick to the North Star: bespoke, warm, authoritative tradition executed with `fantasy.co` polish.
- **Tone of Voice Curation:** Governs the exact lexicon the website uses. It prohibits cheap sales language ("Call now for a free quote!") and replaces it with elite framing ("Schedule a consultation to discuss your vision").
- **Sub-Brand Generation Framework:** When creating child instances (e.g. Cochrane Tile Masters), this document ensures the sub-service completely inherits the elite legacy of the parent brand without dilution. 

## When Should the System Consult This?
Code assistants and AI systems must consult this document whenever asked to:
1. Act as the final check for any major aesthetic overhaul or global color/typography change.
2. Generate base utility classes (like Tailwind presets or CSS variables) to ensure they map to the correct psychological feeling (luxury, durability).
3. Validate if a piece of copywriting aligns with the Master brand tone.
